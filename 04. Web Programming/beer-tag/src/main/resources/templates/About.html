About Beer Tag

Welcome to Beer Tag – the ultimate platform for beer lovers to explore, track, and share their beer journey. Whether you're just starting to explore craft brews or you're a seasoned aficionado, Beer Tag is designed to elevate your experience with each sip!

Our Mission

At Beer Tag, we’re passionate about connecting people through their love of beer. We believe every beer tells a story – from the rich history of its brewery to the unique flavors and aromas in each glass. Our mission is to make it easy for everyone to record their beer experiences, discover new brews, and join a community of beer enthusiasts who share their excitement.

What You Can Do with Beer Tag

Discover New Brews: Access a wide selection of beers, from local microbreweries to international favorites, each with in-depth information and reviews.

Track Your Tasting Journey: Keep a personal record of the beers you’ve tried, rate them, and add tasting notes to remember your favorites.

Connect with the Community: Follow friends, share recommendations, and engage with beer lovers around the world. Find Beer Events and Breweries: Stay updated on local events, tap takeovers, and new releases, and easily locate breweries near you.

Join Us on This Journey

Beer Tag is more than just a tracking tool – it’s a community dedicated to celebrating the art of beer. We’re here to make your beer journey even richer and to help you discover new favorites along the way.

Cheers to great beers and even better company!
